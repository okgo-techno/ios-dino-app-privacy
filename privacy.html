1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the ios-repair-shop-app (the “App”). This App is designed for use by authorized repair shops and service personnel only, for the purpose of vehicle diagnostics, maintenance, and related after-sales operations.

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with it.

2. Data We Collect

Depending on how the App is used and how it is configured by your organization, we may collect the following categories of data:

2.1 Account and Identification Data

  • Repair shop or dealer identifier (e.g., shop ID, branch code)
  • User account information (e.g., technician ID, username, role)
  • Basic contact information of the repair shop (e.g., shop name and location)

2.2 Vehicle and Diagnostic Data

  • Vehicle identifiers (e.g., VIN, license plate number, internal vehicle ID)
  • Vehicle model, configuration, and status information
  • Diagnostic trouble codes, error codes, ECU status, and diagnostic session data
  • Battery or power system information (e.g., state of charge, health indicators, usage history)
  • Maintenance and repair records created via the App

2.3 Device and Connectivity Data

  • Bluetooth device identifiers and connection status related to vehicle communication modules
  • App version, operating system version, and device model
  • Network status and connectivity information (e.g., online/offline state, basic network diagnostics)

2.4 Logs and Diagnostic Information

  • Application logs and error logs generated during operation of the App
  • Diagnostic session logs related to communication between the App, the vehicle, and backend services
  • Information needed to investigate issues, improve stability, and ensure secure operation

2.5 Optional or Organizational Data

In some deployments, your organization may configure the App to collect additional data types, or to link vehicle data with customer information stored in other systems. In such cases, your organization is responsible for ensuring that such use complies with applicable privacy and data-protection laws and providing any necessary notices to end customers.

3. How We Use the Data

We use the data described above for the following purposes:

  • Vehicle diagnostics and repair: To read vehicle status, retrieve diagnostic trouble codes, and assist technicians in troubleshooting and maintenance.
  • Maintenance records: To create and manage repair reports, maintenance histories, and related service documentation.
  • Service quality and safety: To improve the safety, reliability, and performance of vehicles and associated services.
  • App stability and security: To analyze logs and error information to debug issues, enhance system stability, and prevent misuse or unauthorized access.
  • Operational and business analysis: To support after-sales operations, such as statistics on service usage, common faults, and repair efficiency, in anonymized or aggregated form where possible.
  • Legal compliance: To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from competent authorities.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Depending on your jurisdiction, we rely on one or more of the following legal bases to process your data:

  • Performance of a contract with your organization (e.g., service or dealership agreement)
  • Legitimate interests in providing and improving after-sales services, vehicle diagnostics, and safety
  • Compliance with legal obligations related to product safety, recalls, and record-keeping
  • Your consent, where required by applicable law

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share certain data with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: We may engage third-party service providers (such as cloud infrastructure, messaging platforms, or analytics providers) to process data on our behalf, strictly for the purposes described in this Policy.
  • Affiliates and partners: We may share data with affiliated companies and authorized partners that participate in after-sales support, warranty handling, and related operations.
  • Legal and regulatory requirements: We may disclose data when required by law, regulation, or governmental request, or to protect our rights, safety, or property, or those of others.

We do not sell personal data. We do not use data collected via this App for targeted advertising.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Retention periods may vary depending on:

  • Legal and regulatory requirements for maintenance and service records
  • Contractual obligations with your organization
  • Operational needs for diagnostics, safety analyses, and service quality improvement

When data is no longer needed, we will take appropriate steps to delete, anonymize, or aggregate it in accordance with applicable law.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures may include encryption in transit, access controls, logging, and regular security reviews.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we strive to protect your data to the best of our ability.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction and your role (e.g., individual technician vs. organization), you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, such as:

  • The right to access and obtain a copy of your personal data
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data, subject to legal and contractual limitations
  • The right to object to or restrict certain types of processing

In many cases, your employer or the organization operating the repair shop manages user accounts and related data. If you use the App as part of your employment, please first contact your organization’s administrator or representative for requests related to access, correction, or deletion.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We take appropriate measures to ensure that any international data transfers comply with applicable data protection laws, including the use of contractual safeguards where required.

10. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for use by children. It is designed for professional use by authorized repair shops and service personnel only. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes have been made will signify your acceptance of the updated Policy.
